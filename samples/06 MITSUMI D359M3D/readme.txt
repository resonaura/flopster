3.5 inch FDD, model MITSUMI D359M3D

Sampled by Shiru, 16.01.2020


Recorded using Shure C606N microphone with Behringer U-Phoria UMC204 sound
interface. The drive had its lid cover in place, but unlike most of my
recordings, the mic has been pointed to a side near the rear end, to get
brighter, less bassy sound. No noise reduction, EQ, or other processing,
or manipulation besides cutting up and looping one-shots has been applied.

This drive has an unusual interface connector, grouped with power plug into
a single block, with most of GND pins missing.
