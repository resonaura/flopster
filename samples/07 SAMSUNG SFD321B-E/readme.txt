3.5 inch FDD, model SAMSUNG SFD-321B/E

Sampled by Shiru, 14.01.2020


Recorded using Shure C606N microphone with Behringer U-Phoria UMC204 sound
interface. The drive had its lid cover in place, the mic has been pointed
into the disk loading slot. No noise reduction, EQ, or other processing, or
manipulation besides cutting up and looping one-shots has been applied. A few
sounds has been cured a little bit in order to fix drive's minor hiccups
during recording (likely due to its unstable work, it would run correctly
once in a few dozens attempts).

Unlike most 3.5 floppy drives, this drive does not feature easily detachable
lid cover, thus no photo of the internals is provided.
